# 🏥 **Pharmacy Sales & Profitability Dashboard – Power BI Project**

A complete **end-to-end Business Intelligence solution** developed to help pharmacy leadership analyze **sales performance, profitability, product contribution, and regional trends** across multiple European countries.

This repository demonstrates expertise in **Power BI, Data Modeling, DAX, Power Query, and Advanced Business Analytics**.

---

# 🌐 **PROJECT OVERVIEW**

This project analyzes the daily transactional data of a multi-country pharmacy distributor.

The dashboard transforms raw data into **decision-ready insights** for:

* Revenue growth optimization
* Margin improvement
* Regional benchmarking
* Product & brand performance
* Promotion effectiveness
* Pharmacy-type comparison
* Resource allocation

The final output is a **manager-friendly, interactive dashboard** supporting strategic and operational planning.

---

# 📂 **DATA SOURCES**

The analysis is built using structured datasets forming a **star-schema model**:

### **1. DimDate** — Calendar table (Date, Month, Year, Seasonality)

### **2. DimGeography** — Country, Region, City hierarchy

### **3. DimPharmacy** — Pharmacy metadata (Type, Location, Size)

### **4. DimProduct** — Category, Brand, Product hierarchy

### **5. FactSales** — Daily sales transactions (Revenue, Units, Margin, PromoFlag)

These tables provide a **360° business view**.

# 📈 **KEY INSIGHTS FROM THE DASHBOARD**

---

## 🌍 **1. Regional Performance**

* Top 3 countries generate ~65% of total revenue
* Certain regions show strong volume but weak margins
* Urban pharmacies dominate sales volume

---

## 💰 **2. Revenue & Margin Analysis**

* High-margin products contribute less than 30% volume
* Several high-volume products show margin risk
* Margin leakage observed in promotional periods

---

## 🏪 **3. Pharmacy-Level Performance**

* Top 10 pharmacies generate ~40% revenue
* Performance gaps exist within the same region
* Rural pharmacies show lower margins due to logistics costs

---

## 📦 **4. Product & Brand Contribution**

* OTC and Wellness categories drive volume
* Prescription products deliver higher margins
* Few brands dominate category revenue (Pareto effect)

---

## 📣 **5. Promotion Impact**

* Promotions increase volume by ~22%
* Non-promotional sales generate higher margins
* Frequent discounts reduce profitability

---

## 🔄 **6. Time-Based Trends**

* Strong seasonality in winter months
* Revenue spikes during flu seasons
* Stable growth trend over quarters

# 📑 **RECOMMENDATIONS**
